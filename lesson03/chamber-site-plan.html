Pocatello Chamber of Commerce -- Site Plan

Site Name:

The name of my site is the Pocatello Chamber of Commerce. The url will be commerce.pocatello.gov The site will be a resource for local businesses and residents to find information about the city of Pocatello, Idaho.

Purpose:

The purpose of the site is to provide information about the city of Pocatello, Idaho. The site will be a resource for local businesses and residents to find information about the city of Pocatello, Idaho.

Scenarios:

A local business owner wants to find information about the city's current economic status. A person is looking for a place to eat. A mom is looking for things to do for the family. A dad is looking for informaion on sporting events. A visitor is looking for information on mountain biking trails. A local is looking on where to float the Portneuf river.

Typography:

I have chosen to use the Roboto font for paragraph typography and PT Serif for heading typography.

Color Scheme:

The color scheme I have chosen is a muted, earthy color scheme. I have chosen to use a muted green as the primary color, with a lighter green as the secondary color. I have also chosen to use a muted brown as an accent color, and a muted yellow as a highlight color.

Wireframes:

I have chosen to use a three column layout for the desktop version of the site. The left column will be a navigation column, the center column will be the main content column, and the right column will be a sidebar column. The mobile version of the site will be a single column layout, with the navigation column at the top of the page, the main content column in the middle of the page, and the sidebar column at the bottom of the page.
