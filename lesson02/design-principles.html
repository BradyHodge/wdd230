Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Lush.io is a good example of Visual Hierarchy. It uses size, scale, and color to draw users' attention to the most important parts of the page.


Hick's Law

Google.com does a very good job of showing Hick's Law in action. The home page does not have a ton of buttons or UI elements, which helps the user start using the website right away.


White Space and Clean Design

Wikipedia.org is infamous for its simplistic design and good use of white space. The UI is clean and easy to navigate.
