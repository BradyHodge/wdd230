Chamber History

The Pocatello Chamber of Commerce was established in 1950 with the goal of fostering a vibrant and supportive business community in Pocatello, Idaho. Over the years, the chamber has grown to include over 500 member businesses across various industries.

Key Milestones

  • 1950: Founded the Pocatello Chamber of Commerce.
  • 1960: Hosted the first annual gala to celebrate local business success.
  • 1970: Launched the first business support initiative to aid small businesses during economic downturns.
  • 1980: Expanded membership to include businesses from neighboring communities.
  • 2000: Introduced the "Business of the Year" award to recognize outstanding contributions to the community.
  • 2020: Launched a digital platform to enhance member engagement and provide resources online.

The Pocatello Chamber of Commerce continues to serve as a vital resource for businesses in Pocatello, offering networking opportunities, educational programs, and advocacy for local business interests.
