Memberships

NP Membership

Recognizing the valuable role non-profit organizations play in our community, the Chamber offers a free membership tier for non-profits. This membership will allow you to connect with like-minded organizations, gain exposure for your cause, and potentially partner with Chamber members to further your mission.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership package is ideal for businesses seeking a solid foundation in the community. Bronze members will enjoy benefits such as Listing in the Chamber directory, Access to member-only discounts, and Networking events.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership package builds upon the benefits of Bronze membership and offers additional advantages to help you grow your business. Silver members will enjoy everything from the Bronze Membership benefits as well as featured listing in the Chamber directory and promotion through Chamber social media channels

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership package is designed for businesses seeking maximum exposure and engagement within the Chamber community. Gold members will receive all the benefits of Silver membership, plus priority listing in the Chamber directory, sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events and exclusive access to business development resources
